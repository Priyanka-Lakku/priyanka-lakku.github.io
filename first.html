Frequently Asked Questions
What is the concept of the portal?

Click on the “Y” icon to the left of “Logout” to get an idea!

How to write about someone?

From the search box, find their profile and write about them. Recommended friends are also shown on your feed!

Can I edit posts which I have written?

You can go to profile page and click on 'WHAT I SAID' tab and click on the 3 dots which appear there to edit or delete the post which you have written.

Can posts written for me be deleted?

Yes, posts written for you can be deleted by you. Go to your profile, click on “What they said …” and you can see the option to delete the post

Guidelines regarding content of the words:

Please refrain from using offensive or derogatory language in your posts. Censored variants like ‘#@%” and even short forms shouldn’t be used.
Please understand that this is an Institute platform and such content cannot be published.
Student Alumni Relations Cell (SARC), Dean ACR Office holds the right to delete, edit and add the content in the final printed book. If you write more than 700 characters, first 700 will be taken.
By using the portal you are agreeing to the above guidelines.
